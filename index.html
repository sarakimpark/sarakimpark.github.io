About Me

Hi!
 I'm Sara — a full stack software engineer with a not-so-linear path into tech. I actually started out as a pre-med student (and even got into medical school!), but during my gap years teaching high school math, I stumbled into programming. What began as a way to make virtual learning more engaging during the pandemic quickly turned into something I couldn't stop thinking about. I realized that coding gives me the creative freedom and problem-solving challenge I've always loved — along with the opportunity to make an impact on a much larger scale, whether in education, healthcare, or beyond. Now, I get to build meaningful, user-focused applications that help people, and I wouldn't have it any other way.

I'm passionate about learning new skills, exploring innovative solutions, and building user-intuitive applications that solve real world problems. When I'm not coding, you can find me traveling, vlogging, event planning, cooking, or exploring Los Angeles as a recent transplant.
